We don’t just make cola—we craft moments of happiness, one fizzy bubble at a time. Coca-Cola is a globally recognized soft drink brand known for its signature sweet and refreshing taste. It was created in 1886 and has become one of the most iconic beverages in the world.

The history of Coca-Cola dates back to 1886 when Dr. John Pemberton, a pharmacist in Atlanta, Georgia, first created the formula. The drink quickly gained popularity and has been a part of global culture for over a century.

Coca-Cola stands for refreshment, happiness, and inclusivity. The brand emphasizes bringing people together, creating moments of joy, and fostering a positive community experience.

Did you know that Coca-Cola was originally marketed as a tonic for various ailments? It is also the world's most popular soft drink and is sold in more than 200 countries worldwide.
